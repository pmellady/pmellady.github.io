Current Research

I am currently working on a Bayesian joint longitudinal model that combines time-to-event models, item response theory models, and growth mixture models into one comprehensive framework. Specifically, I am implementing soft Bayesian additive regression trees and Albert and Chib data augmentation with latent subject classification to study the relationship between sleep hygeine and cardiovascular disease death outcomes.
